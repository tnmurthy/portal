📥 Source Systems

All operational databases and external data sources that feed the datalake

🔄 Ingestion Layer

AWS Glue, DMS, and EventBridge handle daily extraction and data ingestion

💾 Datalake Zones

Raw → Transform → Curate progression with metadata catalog at each stage

📊 Analytics & Reporting

Altair as primary reporting, Power BI/SSRS for legacy, ML models on curated data

🔐 Governance & Quality

Lake Formation for access, Deequ for quality rules, CloudWatch for monitoring

⏰ Schedule & SLA

Daily ingestion 1-7 AM IST, reports available by 8 AM IST for business users
